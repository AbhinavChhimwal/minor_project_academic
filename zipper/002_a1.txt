In today’s world, technology plays a vital role in everyday activities, influencing communication, education, and work. Devices like smartphones and computers provide quick access to vast amounts of information and enable people to stay connected globally. Tasks such as online shopping, digital payments, and remote learning have become simple and time-saving. Despite these advantages, overdependence on technology may limit social interaction and affect mental health. Maintaining a healthy balance between digital usage and real-life engagement is crucial. When used thoughtfully, technology can enhance efficiency while still allowing individuals to enjoy meaningful relationships and a balanced lifestyle.
