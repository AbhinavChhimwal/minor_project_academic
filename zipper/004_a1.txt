The influence of technology in daily life has grown significantly, affecting communication, learning, and productivity. Devices such as smartphones and laptops make it easy to access information instantly and stay connected with people worldwide. Activities like online shopping, digital banking, and virtual education save both time and effort. On the downside, too much dependence on technology can lead to reduced social interaction and mental stress. Therefore, finding a balance between digital engagement and real-world experiences is essential. Using technology in a mindful way helps individuals stay efficient while maintaining strong relationships and overall well-being.
