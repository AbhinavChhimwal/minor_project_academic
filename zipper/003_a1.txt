Modern technology has transformed the way people live, work, and interact with one another. With the help of smartphones, computers, and the internet, accessing information and communicating across the globe has become effortless. Everyday activities like banking, shopping, and education are now faster and more convenient. However, relying too much on digital tools can reduce personal interactions and negatively impact mental well-being. It is important to strike a balance between online and offline life. Responsible use of technology can increase productivity and convenience while ensuring that human connections and overall health are not compromised.
