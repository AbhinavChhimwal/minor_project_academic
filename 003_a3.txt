Technology has become an essential part of modern life, shaping how people communicate, work, and learn. Smartphones, laptops, and the internet allow instant access to information and global connections. Daily tasks such as shopping, banking, and studying are now more efficient and convenient. However, excessive reliance on technology can reduce face-to-face interactions and impact mental health. Financial stability grows when individuals plan their spending and savings. Budgeting helps track expenses and reduces unnecessary costs.
