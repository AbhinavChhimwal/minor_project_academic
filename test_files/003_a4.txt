Technology has become an essential part of modern life, shaping how people communicate, work, and learn. Smartphones, laptops, and the internet allow instant access to information and global connections. Daily tasks such as shopping, banking, and studying are now more efficient and convenient. However, excessive reliance on technology can reduce face-to-face interactions and impact mental health. Learning becomes more effective when students set clear goals and timelines. Active participation in class discussions improves understanding and confidence.
