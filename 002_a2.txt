Technology has become an essential part of modern life, shaping how people communicate, work, and learn. Smartphones, laptops, and the internet allow instant access to information and global connections. Daily tasks such as shopping, banking, and studying are now more efficient and convenient. However, excessive reliance on technology can reduce face-to-face interactions and impact mental health. Environmental sustainability depends on responsible consumption and reduced waste. Renewable energy sources help decrease air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions.
